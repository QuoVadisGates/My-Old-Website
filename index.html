Journey

Studying Economics as a minor and majoring in Philosophy at Morehouse College in Atlanta, Ga. I found myself more interested in the computing industry then those I studied. Trying to ignore this interest because of the expensive degree I'd paid for, I went into management. Despite achieving in this area, I could no longer ignore that most of my free time was spent in the computing space. Found myself researching: how computers work, the programs that make using them people friendly, and most interesting was their ability to make life easier. So I decided to learn, which later advanced into teaching others.
